A COLONIAL LUNCHEON IN JAIPUR

Jaipur, India.

The directive from the client was to create an Anglo Indian-inspired colonial luncheon at their home in the center of Jaipur. Guests entered into a celadon green and white striped entry marquee and walked down a series of boxed arches with fountains leading to the Gin Tent, luncheon and afternoon tea service. Every fabric, cushion, piece of furniture and architectural detailing was custom made. Over 100 feet of vegan food stations were set up for the luncheon, ending with a classic English afternoon tea. The secret garden boasted a photo booth, massage station, fortune tellers and a glam bar!

CASTIGLION DEL BOSCO

Tuscany, Italy

Castiglion del Bosco, a vision of endless natural beauty and luxury in Buonconvento, Italy served as the stunning backdrop to an extravagant weekend-long Tuscan wedding. Guests ascended stone steps lined with violinists to a remote ceremony lush with wildflowers and greenery, where the bride and groom wed to the sounds of a live orchestra. An exquisite tented reception followed, with guests seated at wooden banquet tables topped with fresh local blooms and romantic lilac-colored décor.

ATLANTIS DUBAI

Crescent Road, Dubai.

Arguably the home to one of the world’s best parties. The Palm Island Dubai and the Atlantis was made the resident venue for our high fying big corporate house. The event had the largest fireworks show in the history of pyrotechnics at its time. 2.5km of land were covered in fireworks to outline the entire Atlasntis hotel. Viewed from below as a guest and from above for the media. The high profile event had a ground-breaking combination of high definition video projection, lighting and pyrotechnics of epic proportions that told the story of corportae Rising from the unimaginable.

It all started with cocktails served from a massive 10ft long sea-dragon that snaked its way through the guests. A formal served dinner for 770 invited guests on a 6,000 sq/ft. custom built deck for guests to enjoy an evening performance and view this amazing spectacle.

AN EVENING AT THE DESERT

Ajmer, Rajasthan

Guests were greeted by a host of drummers at this desolated desert in the city of Ajmer for the annual Ambassador's Charity Gala and, upon entering, were enveloped by terracotta, burnt orange, black and ivory dècor. Tables were covered in authentic Rajasthani mudcloth fabric and punctuated by centerpieces covered in hand-woven paper. Respected guests were seated in an elevated VIP section while various performer's each gave stunning performances.

DINNER AT JAIGARH FORT

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The venue for this spectacular night was Jaigarh Fort, set in the mountains about 40 mins outside of Jaipur . While the guests were of Indian origin, they lived in Hong Kong. We told their story of a journey down the Orient River starting in Hong Kong and making its way down the river to Bollywood, ending the night in a 1940’s inspired Opium Den. They entered the party through a series of 20 giant archways with drummers and fire pots, which got progressively smaller as guests walked through.

Stilt walkers lead guests into the second courtyard for Asian delights served from Dhows and enjoyed amazing entertainment. The journey continued to the third courtyard for a Bollywood-styled Sangeet clad in orange, pink and purple. Following the Sangeet guests made their way to the last courtyard, a custom-made club called Club Supra. The 1940’s opium den-inspired night club had sexy red fringe and red lighting, with a dance floor outlined in hanging black fringe. Sultry opium beds surrounded the courtyard for hookah and relaxation, while other guests danced and mingled late into the night.

THE CHATTARPUR OCEAN CLUB

Chattarpur, Delhi

Held at the Chattarpur Executive Club in the heart of Delhi, the bride and groom hosted a gorgeous wedding ceremony on the farm for 575 friends and family members. Post nuptials, guests were showered with colorful streamers from confetti cannons and led by a gospel choir to enjoy refreshing cocktails in an after-party tent. A fabulous performance by Atif set the tone for the remainder of the evening.
